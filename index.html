Making sushi simple

Salmon

At first glance, the process of making sushi can seem daunting. When you sit at the bar and watch the chef prepare you roll you see his hands are almost a blur. The precision with which he uses his knife seems surgical and what was once a disparate of ingredients, put together in a specific order with best practice standards will invariably result in the product that will undoubtably impress your peers.

The goal of each recipe you find will be to use the same ingredients but with different easily mastered techniques you will be able to make 3 unique recipes.

  • Nigiri Sushi
  • Salmon Poke Bowl
  • Salmon Sushi Roll
